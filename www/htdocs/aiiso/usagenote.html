Using AIISO alone

AIISO on its own can be used to describe the organisational structure of an academic institution, it's constituent parts such as faculties, schools and departments. 


	@prefix aiiso: 
	@prefix broadminster: 
	
	broadminster:
		a aiiso:Institution;
		foaf:name "Broadminster University";
		aiiso:?? broadminster:schools/law;
		.
		
	broadminster:schools/law
		a aiiso:School;
		foaf:name "Broadminster Law School";
		aiiso:?? broadminster:;
		.
This describes Broadminster University and the Law School within it. AIISO alone
